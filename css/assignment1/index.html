About Us

We offer full time daycare service in a warm and caring setting. We provide 2 snacks and a lunch that is catered in a nut and pork free environment. We take the children outside everyday for physical activity and we have an adjoining park. It's offered to the children a variety of activities throughout the day based on their interests and we also have specific times for group activity, arts & crafts, as well as many other learning experiences. We welcome you into our daycare and look forward to provide your child with a fun and safe learning environment.

Admission Condition: Toilet trained

Educational Program

Our educational approach is based on the program given by the Ministere de la Famille : Meeting Early Childhood Needs

In this same spirit, the center offers a comprehensive educational program on fundamental values promoting the development of five areas of development of each individual. It will be decorated with thematic and cultural field trips. Different themes will be set up to allow your child to be creative and master their learning.
